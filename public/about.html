About the Food Mart

Our Focus is the Families

The Food Mart is the quintessential family store focused on the customer. This family’s vision has grown and expanded to now encompass stores in certain parts of Zambia. Now more than ever, we understand that you need reliability and support from your community. The Food Mart is a much-loved brand, valued within society, built on a genuine desire to make life better for our customers and to make a positive contribution to the communities in which we live and work. Our strong and unique family values guide the business for more than, providing a solid foundation for growth, innovation, service excellence and generosity.

What We Offer

Our offer to customers focuses on groceries, clothing and general merchandise, but also includes additional value-added services to cater for our customers’ expectations and evolving needs. To ensure a convenient and accessible shopping experience the Group operates across multiple store formats.

Our Values

Health

You care about your health, and we think that’s pretty great. That’s why we promise to provide only the freshest products and ingredients so you can feel your best.

Local

Sourcing local strengthens the Zambian food system and connects you with unique, hard-to-find items made by dedicated farmers and foodmakers in your community.

Trust

We're proud to partner with local farmers we know and love. We take pride in the relationships we build with our partners and our customers.

Convenience

With both weekly and bi-weekly delivery options, we make healthy living as easy as possible.

Transparency

Food sourcing, ingredient lists, and allergen concerns are important to us. When it comes to our products,there are no secrets.

Community

Our passion started with food, but it’s fueled by people. Organic produce is something we are serious about for our customers health.

Why the Food Mart

Based in Zambia, the Food Mart was established in 2020 to bring local, organic, and fresh groceries straight to your doorstep. This company was built to create a positive experience for people who are committed to their health, happiness, and community. The Food Mart is your one stop shop to fill your pantry with the foods you love, made by farmers and food makers you can trust.
